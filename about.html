About CampusBites

Your trusted partner for delicious campus meals

Our Story

CampusBites was born out of a simple idea: students deserve access to hot, delicious meals without having to leave their hostel rooms, especially during late-night study sessions or busy exam periods.

Founded in 2025 by a group of college students who understood the struggle of finding good food on campus, CampusBites has grown into the most trusted food delivery service for students. We partner with the best local restaurants and campus eateries to bring you a diverse menu of mouth-watering options.

What started as a small operation delivering to a single hostel has now expanded across the entire campus, serving hundreds of hungry students every day. Our mission remains the same: to make delicious food accessible, affordable, and just a click away.

Our Mission

At CampusBites, we're on a mission to revolutionize campus dining. We believe that every student should have access to quality food that doesn't break the bank.

We're committed to:

  • Providing fast and reliable delivery service
  • Offering affordable prices tailored for student budgets
  • Ensuring food quality and hygiene standards
  • Supporting local restaurants and campus vendors
  • Creating a seamless ordering experience
